Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein.

This edition was created for the course Text as data which is a part of the masters degree Digital Text Analysis.

On this page you will find the transcription of the manuscripts of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein and it shows the modifications by both Percy Bysshe shelley and Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley.

The source material of this project comes from The Shelley-Godwin Archive and the The Bodleian Library
